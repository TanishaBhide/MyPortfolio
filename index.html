Crop Prediction using Genetic Algorithm

A genetic algorithm is an optimization technique, that simulates the process of natural selection based on the idea of "survival of the fittest". A solution space is generated where the selected parameters compete and adapt using selection, crossover and mutation techniques.Heuristics guide the evolution and as the individuals evolve over multiple generations, the model approaches towards optimum value.
Key features:
.This novel model solely utilizes Genetic algorithms for prediction.
. It analyzes diverse data inputs—including soil attributes (pH and N, P, K values) and weather variables (like temperature, humidity etc.)—to deliver reliable predictions tailored to specific crops.
.This model empowers farmers with actionableinsights, by providing optimum value of parameters apart from the most ideal crop.
. Achieved an impressive accuracy of 93-95%.
